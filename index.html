About Quantix Silicon

Egypt's First Semiconductor APT Facility

Our Vision

Quantix Silicon is an Egyptian semiconductor company pioneering the Assembly, Packaging, and Testing (APT) of CPU and GPU products under the brand name Q.core One.

Our Business Model

We operate using a fabless + OSAT (Outsourced Semiconductor Assembly and Test) business model:

  • CPU and GPU designs developed or licensed by Quantix Silicon
  • Wafer fabrication outsourced to international foundries (TSMC, Samsung, GlobalFoundries)
  • Local assembly, packaging, and testing in Egypt
  • Advanced manufacturing without wafer fabrication infrastructure

Our Mission

Reduce dependency on fully imported processors

Improve cost efficiency through local operations

Enhance supply chain reliability

Support national technological growth
